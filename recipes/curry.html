Quick Vegan Curry

Description

This vegan curry recipe is full of healthy vegetables, big flavor,
and it takes under 25 minutes! The perfect fast and easy dinner.

Ingredients

  • Yellow onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Spinach
  • Chickpeas
  • Peas
  • Olive oil
  • Fire roasted tomatoes
  • Curry powder and cumin
  • Coconut milk

Steps

  1. Cook the rice: If serving with rice, start the basmati rice or use an instant pot
  2. Prep the vegetables: Dice the onion and bell peppers. Mince the garlic. Peel and mince the ginger.
  3. Saute the veggies: In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium high heat. Add the onion and peppers saute for 5 minutes.
    Add the garlic, ginger, and spinach and saute for 2 minutes until the spinach is fully wilted.
  4. Simmer: Carefully pour in the crushed tomatoes and their juices, then add the curry powder, cumin, salt, chickpeas, peas, and coconut milk.
    Cook for 5 minutes until bubbly. Remove from the heat. If desired, stir in a little more coconut milk. Serve with rice.
